My Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Spagetti
  • Chicken Breasts
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Chicken Sauce
  • Cooking Oil
  • Pot
  • Frying Pan

Steps

  1. Pour water until about slightly more than half of the pot is filled
  2. Boil the water
  3. Once boiled, put about 90g of spaghetti, and about 50g of salt, and a bit of butter
  4. Put the lid on the pot
  5. Pour oil on the frying pan and wait for smoke to appear
  6. Flatten a chicken breast and season with salt and pepper
  7. Put the chicken breast in the frying pan and coat it with cooking oil
  8. Give the chicken breast and spaghetti about 9 minutes, in which you flip the chicken and stir the spaghetti occasionally
  9. After 9 minutes, turn off the stove
  10. Pour out the water in the pot until there is a small amount remaining and then mix it with spaghetti sauce
  11. Put the chicken breasts and the spaghetti in a bowl
  12. You are now done.
