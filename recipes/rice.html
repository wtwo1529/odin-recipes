My Rice and Chicken Breasts Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cooking Oil
  • Pot
  • Frying Pan

Steps

  1. Put however much rice you would like into the pot
  2. Continuously pour enough water until it covers the rice and then filter out the water until there are no more white particles in the water once filled
  3. Put your hand ontop of the rice and pour enough water to cover your hand
  4. Boil the pot with the lid on until the water is completely vaporised
  5. In the meanwhile, cover the pot in oil until steam appears
  6. Flatten a chicken breast and season it will pepper and salt
  7. Place the chicken in the pot and coat it with cooking oil
  8. Give the chicken about 8-9 minutes and regularly flip it
  9. Add sauce to the chicken when done and you are done
