Scrambled Egg

Description

Ingredients

  • Bowl or Cup
  • Egg
  • Stirring Tool
  • Salt
  • Cooking Oil
  • Frying Pan

Steps

  1. Crack two eggs into a cup
  2. Stir until the egg yolks are completely mixed
  3. Season with salt
  4. Pour oil on a frying pan until steam appears
  5. Pour the mixture (in the cup) into the pan
  6. Constantly mix the egg for about a minute
